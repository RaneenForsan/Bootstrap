Who we are?

Excellence LLC,established in 2012, is a fast growing company with a clear vision focusing on improving people’s lives through the delivery of safe and reliable yet fast and comfortable elevators and escalators services.

Excellence has made a promise to contribute to the growth of People’s Mobility Industry in Jordan by providing superior and exceptional wide range of offerings. We start with a big smile and passion to provide the best support to our clients. We take care of their elevators and escalators through routine maintenance jobs, avail spare parts swiftly, and respond promptly to their emergency requests.

Our services extended to various types of sectors such as residential buildings and towers, hospitals, hotels, banks and others. We were able to successfully deliver upgrade projects and win lots of maintenance contracts for Schindler products and others during our first year of operation.

We have chosen the name Excellence because our commitment to operating excellence inspires everything we do. Our team of dedicated and talented employees strives to leave a mark in the elevators industry in Jordan and gain and retain the confidence of our customers in Schindler Products.

Our Team:

Our team enjoys a unique combination of skills, specializations, and experiences. Every member of our Management Team provides leadership and works closely with other teams to ensure the delivery of quality service and support.

We believe the true capital of any service company is its people. Therefore, we invest in our people through continuous training and coaching
