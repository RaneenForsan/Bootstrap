Services



Installation and Upgrades
Our installation team has installed hundreds of elevators and escalators during their vast working experience. They have the necessary knowledge and the practical hands-on. Furthermore, we have created a methodology that emphasizes on implementing the standardized procedures to ensure safety in every installation performed.

Maintainance
Safety First is not just a slogan we say, rather we put into Action. To guarantee safety and to get the best performance and longest lifespan of our products, we provide top quality and world class maintenance solutions. We have standard as well as customized schemas and plans that fit all customer requirements.


Repairs
Our team has encountered and resolved almost all types of defaults through their extensive experience. Their competency allows them to identify the source of issues quickly and suggest the best resolution. They follow our internal strict fixing procedure in terms of analyzing the cause, applying the required repair, testing the equipment before put back into operation, documenting the case and obtain customer feedback.

Emergency Support
We are just a phone call away! We have a dedicated 24-hour 7-day a week hotline to receive all types of emergency issues. Our team is well trained on how to deal with crisis and eliminate panic. They are fully equipped all the time to respond to and act with emergencies.
